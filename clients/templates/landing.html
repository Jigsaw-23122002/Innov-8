Why INNOV-8 ? Current Need

It is very important for young students to get a platform to display their innovations. Current school systems do not have an integrated environment for projects,seminars and exhibitions. Sometimes, various students aren't aware about what they can build and how much impact they can create by building projects.

INNOV-8 basically bridges the gap between the available opportunities and the dedicated students waiting to bring their thought to reality.
